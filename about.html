We Are
The Beach Episode

"Not your average hot mess."

The Beach Episode is made up of seven students currently studying at the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts. Dan, Sean, Dakota, and Jody all met each other at Berklee's table top gaming club during their first week of freshman year. After talking about putting together a band that would focus on playing music from their favorite video game's and anime, they reached out to a few of their friends and ultimately formed the group that makes up the band today. The goal of the band is to play interesting/original arrangements of video game and anime music for audiences at conventions and other venues.
